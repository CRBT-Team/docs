Allows you to get any of the information on the CRBT user.

## Context

The `<crbt>` tag is globally available and does not require a context.

## Syntax

<Tabs groupId="io">
  <TabItem value="input" label="Input">
    <CodeBlock>&lt;crbt.mention&gt; is the best bot!!!</CodeBlock>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="output" label="Output">
    <DiscordMessages>
      <DiscordMessage {...profiles.crbt}>
        <DiscordCommand slot="reply" {...profiles.clembs} command="/the-truth"></DiscordCommand>
        <DiscordMention>CRBT</DiscordMention> is the best bot!!!
      </DiscordMessage>
    </DiscordMessages>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Properties

The `<crbt>` tag inherits most properties from [`<user>`](./user), besides any properties related to Economy.
